Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

My design process typically involves four key phases: research, design, prototype, and test. I start by understanding user needs, move on to creating wireframes and visual design, then build interactive prototypes, and finally gather user feedback to improve the final product.

What tools and software do you use for UX design?

I mainly use tools like Figma for UI/UX design, Adobe XD for prototyping, and Notion or Miro for collaborative research and journey mapping. I also use Google Analytics and Hotjar to understand user behavior.

How do you measure the success of your UX designs?

Success is measured through both qualitative feedback (user interviews, usability tests) and quantitative metrics (conversion rates, bounce rates, task success rate). I also track how effectively users can achieve their goals using the product.
